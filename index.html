Projects

DataSafe - De-identification Initiative

DataSafe is a strategic initiative aimed at safeguarding sensitive information within our data repositories. The project's primary goal is to ensure personal identifiers are effectively anonymized or removed, thereby preserving user privacy and ensuring compliance with data protection regulations. Leveraging state-of-the-art de-identification algorithms and tools, DataSafe will scan, detect, and transform identifiable data into a format that cannot be easily traced back to individual subjects. The outcome will be a dataset that retains its utility for research and analysis but without compromising the confidentiality of the individuals represented within.

BlueDoc - Unified Database Search Engine

Designed to seamlessly integrate and reference the myriad databases within the company, BlueDoc offers a one-stop solution for data retrieval and reference. With an intuitive user interface and robust back-end algorithms, users can effortlessly pinpoint and access information spread across different data reservoirs. Beyond simplifying data searches, BlueDoc ensures optimal productivity, enhancing the decision-making processes across all levels of Blue Cross.
